- Title :Giovanni's Room.

- Author :James Baldwin.

- Description of the book :"Giovanni's Room" is a novel written by James Baldwin about a young American man named David who is living in Paris and struggling to come to terms with his sexuality. He becomes involved with an Italian bartender named Giovanni and the two of them develop a tumultuous relationship as David tries to understand his own desires and come to terms with his identity. The novel explores themes of love, identity, and the search for self-acceptance in a society that does not always understand or accept non-traditional relationships. The story is set against the backdrop of the bohemian society of 1950s Paris and touches on issues of race and class as well.
